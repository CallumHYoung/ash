# Ideas

A scratchpad for design ideas to consider. Not a roadmap — things here may
be picked up, reshaped, or dropped entirely.

## Silent sanity decay

The character loses sanity silently as they descend. The air is sickening
the deeper they go.

- No visible meter. The player should feel it, not read it off a number.
- Possible expression: subtle desaturation, low-frequency heartbeat sound,
  occasional whispered words the NPC said earlier looping back, distant
  scraping, the screen edges starting to pulse.
- Could couple to depth (linear) or to time-spent-low (so resting at the
  bottom is worse than passing through quickly).
- Hitting a checkpoint platform might bleed a little sanity back — a
  micro-rest, a held breath.
- At thresholds, things start happening: torches that aren't really there,
  the rope going slack for a frame, footsteps behind the player.
